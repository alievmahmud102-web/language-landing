# Project overview

## What we are building

An **integrated customer acquisition and management system** for education centers: **conversion-focused website**, **CRM** for inquiries and students, **analytics dashboard**, **light admin**, and **integrations** (Telegram, web forms; room for more APIs).

## Who it is for

**B2B**, small and medium **education businesses**: language schools, IT training, exam preparation centers.

## Stage and goals

- **Stage**: MVP with first paying customers.
- **3–6 month goals**:
  - Reach **stable deals** in roughly the **$700–$2,000** range per engagement (adjust when pricing is finalized).
  - Produce **3–5 case studies** with measurable outcomes.
  - Position and harden the product as a **coherent system**, not a one-off site.

## How we work

- **Solo founder** plus AI-assisted development (**Cursor**).
- **Monorepo** for simpler coordination and deployment.
- **Code** in English; **product UI** in Russian.
