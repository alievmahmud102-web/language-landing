# Product

## Value proposition

We help education centers **turn more inquiries into enrollments** by combining:

1. A **landing experience** optimized for parents and students to submit a request.
2. A **CRM** that tracks every inquiry and learner without spreadsheet chaos.
3. A **dashboard** that shows volume, conversion, and **lead sources** so marketing and sales decisions are grounded in data.
4. **Integrations** so leads do not die in inboxes (Telegram, forms; extensible to other channels).
5. A **simple admin** to run the day-to-day without IT overhead.

## Core capabilities (MVP scope)

| Area | Capability |
|------|------------|
| Acquisition | Fast, mobile-first landing; clear CTA; SEO-aware structure |
| CRM | Lead list, status pipeline, notes, assignment basics, link to student record when won |
| Analytics | Period metrics, funnel counts, source breakdown (UTM / tags) |
| Integrations | Form endpoints → lead creation; Telegram notifications or bot handoff (as implemented) |
| Admin | Manage courses/landing snippets/users as scoped by version |

## Out of scope (unless a ticket says otherwise)

- Full generic CRM replacement for enterprise sales teams.
- Multi-tenant marketplace for unrelated industries without customization.

## Differentiation (one line)

We do not sell “just a website”; we ship a **system that brings and processes clients** end-to-end for training businesses.

## Success metrics (examples)

- Time from inquiry to first contact (operational).
- Lead → trial / enrollment conversion (business).
- Share of leads with **known source** (marketing hygiene).
