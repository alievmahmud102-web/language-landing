# FAQ (sales & onboarding)

**Audience**: center owners and admins. Prepare **Russian** customer-facing answers; English here as source copy.

## Product / positioning

**Q: Is this just a website?**  
A: No. The website is the **front door**. You also get a **CRM** for every inquiry and a **dashboard** for conversion and sources—so the site pays rent in real business outcomes.

**Q: We already use Tilda / WordPress.**  
A: Those are fine publishing tools. The gap is usually **pipeline**: leads in chats, no stages, no numbers. We replace that chaos with a **single flow** built for centers.

**Q: We already have AmoCRM / Bitrix.**  
A: If you love it, we can discuss integration scope—but many SMB centers drown in setup. Our default is a **lighter** stack aligned to **one** funnel. If CRM sync is required, we price discovery separately.

## Delivery

**Q: How long does implementation take?**  
A: Typical **Launch** tier is on the order of **2–6 weeks** depending on content readiness and integrations—confirm in proposal.

**Q: Who writes the texts?**  
A: You approve facts and tone; we can work from your draft or include limited copy help if purchased.

**Q: What do you need from us?**  
A: Programs and prices, photos or permission to use stock, access to domain/DNS, list of lead channels, decision-maker for approvals.

## Tech & security

**Q: Where is data stored?**  
A: PostgreSQL on infrastructure we agree (VPS / provider). Document region for privacy conversations.

**Q: Is parent data safe?**  
A: We apply baseline security: HTTPS, server validation, authenticated admin. Legal privacy text remains the center’s responsibility with counsel if needed.

## Pricing

**Q: Why not hire a freelancer for $300?**  
A: You might get a page. You rarely get a **measured pipeline**. Price matches **scope + responsibility + iteration** toward enrollments.

**Q: What after launch?**  
A: Optional retainer for monitoring, small improvements, and monthly metrics review—optional, not forced.

## Support

**Q: What if something breaks?**  
A: Define warranty window in offer (e.g. 30 days critical bugfix). After that: retainer or hourly as agreed.
