# Sales script

**Tone**: direct, expert, no jargon wall. **Language**: Russian with client; this doc in English for internal use—mirror in RU when calling.

## 1. Opening (context)

> “Thanks for taking the time. I focus on education centers—language, IT, exam prep. The goal isn’t a prettier site alone; it’s **more enrollments from the inquiries you already get**.”

## 2. Discovery (questions)

- “Walk me through what happens **in the first 10 minutes** after a parent writes you.”
- “Where do inquiries land—DMs, phone, Google form?”
- “Do you know **which channel** brings the best students, not just the most noise?”
- “What would success look like in **60 days**—numbers?”

## 3. Value bridge

> “We’ll put in a **conversion landing**, a **simple CRM** so nothing falls through, and a **dashboard** for sources and stages. **Telegram** and your **forms** feed straight in so admins aren’t copy-pasting.”

## 4. Differentiation (short)

> “Studios sell pages. Builders sell templates. CRMs sell seats and complexity. I sell a **small system** that matches how a center actually works.”

## 5. Handle price impatience

> “Price ties to scope—**Launch** vs **Growth**. I’ll send a written scope so there’s no surprise line items.”

## 6. Close micro-commit

> “Send your current link and a screenshot of how you track leads today. I’ll reply with a **15-min** assessment and whether we’re a fit.”

## 7. After call

- Log outcome in your CRM (meta, but do it).
- Send **commercial offer** (`commercial-offer.md` template) within agreed SLA.
