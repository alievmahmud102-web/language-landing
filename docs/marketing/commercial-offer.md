# Commercial offer (template)

> Adapt per client. Keep **Russian** for client-facing PDF if that’s your market; this file is English as master outline—translate headings as needed.

---

## Cover

- Client legal name, contact person.
- Your legal name / sole prop details (fill when ready).
- Date, validity (e.g. 14 days).

## 1. Summary

2–4 sentences: problem agreed in discovery + outcome-oriented promise (system: attract → process → measure).

## 2. Scope of work

**Included**

- Next.js **landing** (sections: hero, programs, proof, FAQ, CTA; mobile-first).
- **CRM**: lead capture, statuses, notes, list views (per agreed spec).
- **Dashboard**: metrics for period ___ (define KPIs).
- **Integrations**: web forms → API; Telegram: ___ (notify / intake—specify).
- **Admin**: ___ (users, org settings, content blocks—specify).
- **Deployment** to agreed hosting; **handover** session (60–90 min) + checklist.

**Not included (unless addendum)**

- Ongoing ad management, full SEO retainer, custom mobile apps, legal/privacy policy drafting (can refer), photography, content beyond agreed hours.

## 3. Timeline

- Week 1: ___  
- Week 2: ___  
- Go-live target: ___

## 4. Client responsibilities

- Text approvals within __ business days.
- Access: domain DNS, existing accounts if migrating content.
- Single point of contact for decisions.

## 5. Investment

| Item | Amount | Terms |
|------|--------|--------|
| Project total | $____ | Deposit ____%, balance ____ |
| Optional retainer | $____ / month | Cancel notice ___ days |

## 6. Assumptions & change requests

- Changes beyond signed scope: estimated separately.
- Third-party fees (domains, paid APIs): per invoice or pass-through as agreed.

## 7. Acceptance

- Definition of done: checklist in Appendix A (link or bullet list).

## 8. Signatures

- Client signature, your signature.

---

**Appendix A — Acceptance checklist (example)**

- [ ] Landing loads on mobile; form submits successfully.
- [ ] Lead appears in CRM with correct source/UTM.
- [ ] Telegram behavior verified.
- [ ] Dashboard numbers match sample test data.
- [ ] Admin can invite second user (if in scope).
