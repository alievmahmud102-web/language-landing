# Pain points

## Operational

- **Leads scattered** across Instagram, WhatsApp, phone, email—no single inbox or status.
- **Slow first response**; hot parents choose the center that answers first.
- **No history**: staff changes and context is lost.
- **Spreadsheet CRM** breaks under volume; errors and duplicates.

## Marketing

- **Website does not convert**; pretty pages but weak CTA or slow mobile.
- **No source visibility**: “We get leads” but cannot say from which channel or campaign.
- **Cannot prove ROI** on ads or partners.

## Competitive fear

- Competitors look more modern online; **trust gap** at first impression.

## Strategic

- Owner wants **predictable enrollment** but tools are disconnected (site vs CRM vs reports).

## How our system maps to pains

| Pain | Product answer |
|------|------------------|
| Scattered leads | Unified CRM + integrations (forms, Telegram) |
| Slow response | Notifications + clear queue; optional future SLA metrics |
| No source data | UTM/tags + dashboard breakdown |
| Weak conversion | Landing built for **requests**, not vanity |
| Disconnected tools | One system: **attract → process → measure** |

## Questions to ask in discovery

1. “Where does a new parent inquiry land today, step by step?”
2. “How fast is first contact on average—honestly?”
3. “What would ‘success’ look like in 60 days—numbers?”
