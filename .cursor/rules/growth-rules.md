# Growth rules

## Positioning

- Sell a **system** (site + CRM + analytics + integrations), not “just a website.” Messaging should tie features to **pipeline outcomes**: more qualified inquiries, faster follow-up, visible sources and conversion.

## Funnel discipline

- Every landing change should map to a **hypothesis** (e.g. higher form start rate) and a **metric** (submissions, cost per lead if paid).

## Experiments

- Prefer small iterative changes over big-bang redesigns unless a task explicitly scopes a redesign.
- Document what was shipped and what to measure in `docs/marketing/` or release notes when it affects GTM.

## Ethics

- No dark patterns; honest claims; GDPR/local privacy awareness when storing parent/student data—align with legal text in commercial materials.

## Economics mindset

- Track assumptions for **CAC**, **deal size**, and **payback**; update `docs/marketing/unit-economics.md` when pricing or channel mix changes materially.
