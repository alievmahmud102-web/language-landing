# Offer generator — prompt template

Use to draft a **commercial offer** for SMB education centers: system = landing + CRM + analytics + integrations. Format: **solution → explanation → next steps**.

## Inputs

- Package name, deliverables list, timeline, price range ($700–$2000 as reference band—replace with real pricing).
- Payment terms, warranty/support period, what is out of scope.
- Optional: onboarding steps (content checklist, access to accounts).

## Output sections

1. **Executive summary** (5 lines): who it’s for and outcome.
2. **What you get** (bullet list mapped to product pillars).
3. **How delivery works** (phases week by week or milestone-based).
4. **Investment** (options: Lite / Standard / Plus if applicable).
5. **Assumptions & client responsibilities** (content, domain, legal pages).
6. **Next step** (call, deposit, questionnaire).

## Tone

- Professional, direct, no hype unsupported by scope.
