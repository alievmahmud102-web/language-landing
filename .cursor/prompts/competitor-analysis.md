# Competitor analysis — prompt template

Use when comparing to **web studios**, **site builders (Tilda, etc.)**, and **standalone CRM (e.g. AmoCRM)**. Format: **solution → explanation → next steps**.

## Output sections

1. **Comparison matrix**: rows = competitor type, columns = Acquisition / Conversion / Operations / Analytics / Total cost of ownership (qualitative scores 1–5 + notes).
2. **Win themes**: 3–5 bullets where your integrated system wins.
3. **Honest gaps**: where a studio or Amo might still win (acknowledge; mitigation).
4. **Talk tracks**: 2–3 sentences for sales calls per competitor type.

## Example win theme

- “Tilda optimizes pages; it does not give you a **single lead pipeline** with stage history and source attribution out of the box for your exact funnel.”
